**Date:** February 4, 2026
**Status:** Manus was acquired by Meta (now part of Meta)
**Market Status:** Launched paid plans March 2025, still in beta as of acquisition

---

## Executive Summary

Manus proved market demand for **task-executing AI agents** (not just chatbots) with a credit-based model starting at $39/mo. Key insight: **Users pay for automation of repetitive, research-heavy admin work** that they understand but hate doing themselves.

**What worked:** Research compilation, recurring reports, simple workflow automation
**What didn't scale:** Complex cross-department workflows, limited integrations, customization constraints

**Critical learning:** The market wants agents that *do things* (create files, schedule, integrate with tools), not just answer questions.

## 2. Pricing & Business Model

### Pricing Tiers (as of March 2025)

| Plan | Price | Credits | Simultaneous Tasks | Key Features |
|------|-------|---------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Free** | $0 | Daily refresh credits | 1 task | Explore small tasks, test platform |
| **Basic** | $39/mo | 3,900/mo + 300 daily refresh | 2 tasks | Core automation, standard support |
| **Plus** | $99/mo | 9,900/mo + 300 daily refresh | 3 tasks | More capacity, faster execution |
| **Pro** | $199/mo | 19,900/mo + 300 daily refresh | 5 tasks | Priority access, highest capacity |

**Credit System:**
- Credits = tokens for task complexity
- Simple queries: low credits
- Complex tasks (coding, slides, web apps, multi-step research): high credits
- Credits DON'T roll over month-to-month (use-it-or-lose-it model)

### Key Business Model Insights

1. **Credit scarcity creates upgrade pressure** â€” users run out of credits mid-month and upgrade
2. **Simultaneous task limit** is the real constraint â€” power users hit this faster than credit limit
3. **Daily refresh credits** (300/day) keep free users engaged without giving away full value
4. **$39/mo entry point** is low enough for solopreneurs, high enough to filter serious users
5. **No per-task pricing** â€” predictable monthly cost appeals to businesses
